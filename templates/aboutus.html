What do we aim at?

Our main aim is to make Covid-19 Vaccine Registration facility accessible to every Indian citizen, so that every Indian can get himself/herself registered on this website for vaccination. Vaccination rate in India has already been increased significantly and this website aims at fully vaccinated India by the end of 2023.

Our website thoroughly explains the registration and vaccination processes due to which there has already been a surge of users. Also, users can easily find the list of vaccination centres nearby which helps them to choose a suitable vaccination centre. Statistics regarding vaccination in various centres is also available so that users can keep themselves updated regarding the same.

Information related to various vaccines is also provided for the users' ease.

There are many rumours against onsite registration for covid vaccine in rural areas. So, this website basically will be an open provision for everyone to get vaccinated.

Also, for those who are keen to know more about the deadly virus, we have provided some articles on our website.
